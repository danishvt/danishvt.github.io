Professional Summary

Who am I

In August 2010, Dani has established the Pango Mobile parking operation in the US as a licensee in PA, MD, DC. He was responsible for the financial and operational localization and the successful implementation of the Pango SAAS (Software as Service) cloud based system for the Pango operation in the US. Raising capital from private investors, establishing the payment processing method while partnering with the second largest Bank in the US - Wells Fargo, and introducing Pango to municipalities and parking operators while structuring Pango’s strategy and CI (Corporate Identity) in the US market. Currently, Pango is operational in several cities in the US and bidding large cities that are expected to join Pango during the next few months. In February 2014, the licensee has been acquired by the Licensor - Pango Mobile Parking LTD Until 2010, for 4 years, Dani was the CEO of Eldan Transportation Group, one of the leading car leasing and rental companies in Israel. Prior to becoming the CEO of Eldan, he served as the Managing Director of the successful Hyundai cars distributorship in Israel for 4.5 years. Before Hyundai, Dani was the Managing Director of SKF distributorship in Israel for 2 years. At the beginning of his professional career, he worked for 8 years as a Mechanical Engineer at Yona Uspiz Electric Motors ltd, the largest electric motors manufacturer in Israel. Dani succeeded in leading these companies to a high level of achievement measured by financial prosperity and by guiding them to be leaders in their respective fields The skills that brought him to this level of accomplishment cover several fields of expertise. Dani holds an M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from the Technion, the Israeli Institute of Technology. He also has an "International Executive MBA" from the Kellogg-Recanati program of the Kellogg School in Evanston, Il, USA. These degrees have contributed to his instructive and analytical managerial profile. This enables him to conduct long term strategies meeting a company's vision and conversely he is able to delve into the details of a business or a project. Over the course of his professional years, Dani had the opportunity to work with diversified groups of colleagues and business partners from many countries such as the US, UK, Belgium, Sweden, Germany, Holland, Japan, S. Korea, China, India, New-Zealand and Israel. With all of them, he successfully used his innate and acquired skills of presentation and negotiation. While in the Israeli army reserves, he served as a Battalion Commander with the rank of LTC, a position that reflected his innate ability to lead people to meet goals and effect missions. Professionalism, excellence and customer satisfaction represent the most important core values which characterize his managerial philosophy. Thus he is highly proficient at entrepreneurship and leading companies to achieve strategic goals. During the last 4 years in Israel, apart from Eldan activities, Dani acted as Director of the Board in two start-up companies and in one mature company, and as Director of the Board at the Israeli Association of Car Rental and Leasing and led activities associated with the government, municipalities and multilateral activities among the association members. Dani was raised and lived in Israel and has traveled worldwide extensively. He is married to Hagit and they have four children.
